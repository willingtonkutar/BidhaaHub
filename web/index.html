About BidhaaHub

Built for grocery and retail shops that need a simple way to control stock, avoid waste, and act faster on supplier decisions.

What this platform does

  • Centralizes products, suppliers, and stock information in one workspace.
  • Highlights low-stock and expiry risks before they cause losses.
  • Supports faster operations with quick actions and searchable records.
